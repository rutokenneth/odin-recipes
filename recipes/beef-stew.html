Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds stewing beef, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for serving

Steps to follow

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef, flour, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook, turning occasionally, until browned on all sides.
  4. Add onion, carrots, celery, and garlic to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, beef broth, red wine (if using), bay leaf, thyme, rosemary, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  6. Simmer for 1 hour, or until beef is tender.
  7. Stir in potatoes and peas and simmer for an additional 15 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
